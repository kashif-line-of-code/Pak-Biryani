Our Story

Discover Our Journey

Our Heritage

Founded in 1985 by Mr. Ahmed Khan, PakBiryani began as a small food stall in the bustling streets of Karachi. With a secret family recipe passed down through generations, Mr. Khan's biryani quickly became the talk of the town.

Today, under the leadership of his grandson, Ali Khan, we continue to honor our heritage while embracing modern culinary techniques. Our commitment to quality and authenticity remains unchanged.

Each biryani is still prepared with the same care and attention to detail that made our founder famous. We source only the finest basmati rice, premium meats, and hand-picked spices to ensure every dish meets our high standards.

Our Journey

1985

Humble Beginnings

Mr. Ahmed Khan starts a small food stall in Karachi with his secret family biryani recipe. The aroma of his cooking quickly attracts loyal customers.

1995

First Restaurant

Due to overwhelming popularity, the first PakBiryani restaurant opens in central Karachi, seating 50 guests and featuring an expanded menu.

2005

Expansion

Three new locations open across Karachi. PakBiryani becomes a household name for authentic Pakistani cuisine in the city.

2015

Third Generation

Ali Khan, grandson of the founder, takes over operations and introduces modern management while preserving traditional recipes.

2025

Online Presence

Launch of our website and online ordering system, bringing authentic Pakistani biryani to customers across the country with nationwide delivery.

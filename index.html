Our Story

Traditional Taste Since 1985

Our journey began in the heart of Karachi, Pakistan, where our founder, Mr. Ahmed Khan, started a small food stall with a passion for authentic Pakistani biryani. Using secret family recipes passed down through generations, he created biryanis that quickly became the talk of the town.

Today, we continue to honor his legacy by using the same traditional methods and premium ingredients. Each biryani is prepared with care, using the finest basmati rice, carefully selected spices, and fresh meat or vegetables.

Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a favorite among biryani lovers. We take pride in bringing the true taste of Pakistan to your table.

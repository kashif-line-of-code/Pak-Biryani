Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery areas?

We deliver across Karachi with free delivery for orders above Rs. 1000. For areas outside our standard delivery zone, a small delivery fee may apply.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery time is 45-60 minutes. During peak hours or special occasions, it may take up to 90 minutes. We always strive to deliver your food hot and fresh.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive catering services for events, parties, and corporate functions. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for catering orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments through JazzCash and EasyPaisa. Online payment options are also available through our website.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian options including Vegetable Biryani, Daal Chawal, and a selection of vegetable curries and appetizers.
